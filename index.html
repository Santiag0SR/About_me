    Who I Am

    My name is Santiago Sánchez Romero, I am currently a student at Practicum by Yandex, learning to become a web developer.

    I work for a Travel Tour Operator specialised in marathons, we take people from South America to the best marathons in the world: New York, Tokyo, London, Berlin, Chicago and Boston. In my actual job I travel with our clients to this places and make sure they only worry about running a good marathon. In between I sell packages and manage logistics. Obviously everything has slowed down due to the COVID-19.

    Web development is something I have always been attracted to. Specially through my brother, he works in the backend part for a big company. I love interacting with the machine and being able to solve problems, I look at it as a puzzle I have to resolve with the tools that I have, I love what I have learned so far, and also love the possibility to be able to work from whenever I want.

    My goal for this course is being able to work at the end of it as a junior web developer. I want to jump career and I feel like this course is going to help me do it!
